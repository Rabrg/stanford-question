The Broncos' defense ranked first in the NFL yards allowed (4,530) for the first time in franchise history, and fourth in points allowed (296).
Defensive ends Derek Wolfe and Malik Jackson each had 5½ sacks.
Pro Bowl linebacker Von Miller led the team with 11 sacks, forced four fumbles, and recovered three.
Linebacker DeMarcus Ware was selected to play in the Pro Bowl for the ninth time in his career, ranking second on the team with 7½ sacks.
Linebacker Brandon Marshall led the team in total tackles with 109, while Danny Trevathan ranked second with 102.
Cornerbacks Aqib Talib (three interceptions) and Chris Harris, Jr. (two interceptions) were the other two Pro Bowl selections from the defense.
